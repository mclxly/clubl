ABOUT US

The Club L International, LLC is registered in the state of Illinois and is a legally authorized private club for international students.

The Club L’s Motto: “Provide unparalleled service to our members with the utmost sincerity”. Based on years of research and study, The Club L has developed a deep understanding for the needs of the International student. We have developed our international service platform based upon our superiority in location, connections, and knowledge, which we then utilize in a way that caters to our clients needs. Our service packages can include: academic help, career advisory, social & life, preferred and ultimate service. The Club L uses the platform of an international private club in order to dedicate us to providing unique and personalized services to our clients to help them better succeed in academia, career placement, and personal networking.

The founders of The Club L are a group of creative, adventurous, and hardworking young adults who graduated from the top universities of United States with Bachelor’s or Master’s degrees. Among our diversified founders, some have already set up their own company in China/United States, some have grown up in the United States, and some have had a successful career in their chosen field thus far. The Club L’s spirit and core competitiveness have attracted many experts from both the fields of education and business to join us. They work to offer devoted services to our members and provide valuable connections to our customers that can eventually lead to the success of The Club L members.
